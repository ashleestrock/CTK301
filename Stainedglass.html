Stained Glass

How I made my piece

Draw the design on paper

Cut out pieces from paper

Pick out glass pieces

Trace paper pieces with sharpie on glass

Score pieces with glass cutter

Break pieces apart with rubber tip glass running pliers

Trim pieces with glass grinder and safety glasses or glasses on

Cut frame

Copper foil piece of glass

Soldering stained-glass

How long

I took me about 2 to 3 months to do because of the curves. If you are just starting I would start with straight edges.
